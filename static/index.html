How It Works

In plain English
How are these odds made?

Every team gets a strength score called Elo. 1500 is average, 2200 and up is world class. Win against a strong team and your score rises. Lose to a weak one and it falls.

  1. 1 Rate every team. We score all 48 nations using 150 years of results, over 49,000 matches. Recent games and World Cup games count the most.
  2. 2 Predict each match. The gap between two teams' scores turns into a likely result and scoreline.
  3. 3 Play it out 30,000 times. We simulate the whole tournament over and over. How often a team wins becomes its title percentage.

Games that have already been played are locked in, so the odds follow the real tournament.
